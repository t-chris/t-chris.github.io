Novel - What Astra Knew

Currently, I'm writing a novelette, novella, novel?... called What Astra Knew. It's set in a not-so-distant future where humanity has just reached interstellar, and they're on the losing side of a war against a microscopic alien race known as theTorellians. I don't draw, but here's an of a domed glass city state known as Fundiny that rests on Saturn's B Ring.

You might be wondering, so does it follow an admiral's exploits against a foreign enemy aboard a gigantic hypercarrier, or the gritty, brutal frontlines of a private's skirmish near the Pleiades Star Cluster? Nope, it follows a "Grave Rider" named Steven, a guy whose job is it to transport the dead back to their family for burial on a long, interplanetary trucking journey; that is, until one of the coffins he's transporting starts moving.
